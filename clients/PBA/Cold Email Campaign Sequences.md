## Usage Notes

- **Keep all follow-ups as replies** to the same thread (re: quick question) — it keeps the conversation feel and increases open rates
- **Icebreaker signals:** recent LinkedIn posts, leadership hires, rebrands, new service launches, funding, speaking appearances
- **Adapt the icebreaker per ICP:** CEOs → strategic moves. CMOs → channel/brand expansion. Brand/Content → content initiative. AI leaders → tech rollout or automation push
